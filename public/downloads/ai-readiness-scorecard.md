## How to Use This Scorecard

This scorecard evaluates your business across five dimensions of AI readiness. For each statement, rate your organization on a scale of 1–5:

- **1** = Not at all / We haven't started
- **2** = Minimal / Just beginning
- **3** = Moderate / Some progress made
- **4** = Strong / Well-established
- **5** = Excellent / Fully mature

Be honest — this tool is for your benefit, not for show. An accurate assessment leads to better recommendations.

## Recommended Next Steps by Level

### If You Scored 160–200 (AI-Ready)

1. Choose your first AI project based on highest ROI potential
2. Set a 30-day implementation timeline
3. Define success metrics before you start
4. Plan for team training alongside deployment
5. Book a strategy session to accelerate implementation

### If You Scored 120–159 (Nearly Ready)

1. Identify your weakest dimension and address it first
2. Clean up your CRM data (if Data Readiness was low)
3. Document your top 5 workflows (if Workflow Maturity was low)
4. Run an AI awareness session for your team (if Team Readiness was low)
5. Upgrade legacy systems (if Infrastructure was low)

### If You Scored 80–119 (Building Foundation)

1. Implement or clean up your CRM as priority one
2. Move to cloud-based systems where possible
3. Document your core business processes
4. Establish basic automation (email rules, form notifications, task assignments)
5. Build team comfort with technology change through small wins

### If You Scored 40–79 (Early Stage)

1. Start with the basics: reliable email, file storage, and a simple CRM
2. Ensure security fundamentals are in place (MFA, backups, endpoint protection)
3. Begin documenting how work gets done in your organization
4. Invest in team technology literacy
5. Set a 6-month goal to reassess readiness

## Dimension Gap Analysis

Your lowest-scoring dimension is your biggest bottleneck. AI readiness is limited by your weakest link:

- **Low Data Readiness** → AI has nothing to work with. Fix this first.
- **Low Workflow Maturity** → AI has nothing to automate. Document and standardize processes.
- **Low Team Readiness** → AI tools won't be adopted. Invest in education and change management.
- **Low Infrastructure** → AI tools can't connect. Modernize your tech stack.
- **Low Opportunity ID** → You'll invest in the wrong things. Spend time on discovery.
